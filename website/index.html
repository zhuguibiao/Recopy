Recopy

Features

Everything you need to manage your clipboard history.

Full-type Support

Plain text, rich text, images, and files — every clipboard format, captured.

Instant Recall

Cmd+Shift+V to summon, arrow keys to navigate, Enter to paste. That fast.

Smart Dedup

SHA-256 hashing detects duplicates automatically. Only the latest copy stays.

Full-text Search

FTS5 with trigram tokenizer. Search in Chinese or English, find anything instantly.

Favorites

Pin frequently used items for quick access. They won't be auto-cleaned.

Non-activating Panel

NSPanel on macOS ensures Recopy never steals focus from your active app.

Themes

Dark and light mode, or follow your system. Switch anytime you want.

Privacy First

All data stored locally in SQLite. Nothing is uploaded, nothing leaves your machine.

How it Works

Three steps. That's all it takes.

1

Copy anything

Text, images, files — Recopy captures it all automatically in the background.

2

Summon Recopy

Press Cmd+Shift+V to open the panel. Browse or search your clipboard history.

3

Paste it back

Select an item and press Enter. Content is pasted seamlessly into your active app.

Under the Hood

Built with performance and privacy in mind.

Local Storage

Your data stays on your machine. SQLite with WAL mode, zero cloud dependency.

Smart Dedup

SHA-256 hashing detects duplicates. Only the latest copy is kept, saving space.

Fuzzy Search

FTS5 + trigram tokenizer. Search in Chinese or English, find anything.
